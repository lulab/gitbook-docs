# How to check the quantity and quality of RNA-seq library

## 1. Library quantity

* Qubit dsDNA kit
* Agilent 2100

## 2. Library quality

* Agilent 2100
* qPCR

Using qPCR primer pairs to target the P5/P7 and spike-in sequences, only the libraries constructed successfully could be amplyfied.
